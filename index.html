IIT KANPUR ROCKETRY & SPACE EXPLORATION TEAM

We are a team of students enthusiastic about building Experimental Rockets & meaningful payloads.

ABOUT US

About us

OUR AIM

Our aim is to ignite the passion for space tech among the campus community by building experimental rockets and meaningful payloads from scratch. We usually participate in competitions like the Spaceport America Cup and Latin America Space Challenge to compete with teams from leading universities around the globe.

Inspired by the Jugnu nano-satellite mission (2008-11) of IIT Kanpur.
